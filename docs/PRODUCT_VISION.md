# SmartFlow Product Vision

## Mission

SmartFlow helps service-based businesses convert customer inquiries into revenue by automatically capturing leads, organizing follow-up tasks, and keeping teams focused on what matters.

## Vision

Build the most accessible automation platform for small and medium service businesses that need reliable lead intake, reminders, and campaign orchestration without technical overhead.

## Target Customer

- Local service providers: contractors, home services, salons, workshops, agencies.
- Small sales teams that rely on inbound customer requests.
- Businesses that want a lightweight CRM plus automated follow-up without heavy enterprise complexity.

## Core Value Proposition

- Automatically collect customer inquiries in one place.
- Organize leads and reminders so nothing falls through the cracks.
- Reduce manual work with email, reminder, and workflow automation.
- Provide clear, actionable visibility into inbound demand.

## Key Differentiators

- Purpose-built for service businesses rather than general-purpose CRM.
- Lean, modular foundation that can expand into integrations without latency.
- Focus on real usage: onboarding, lead capture, reminders, and simple automations.
- Architecture designed for secure multi-tenant scaling through Supabase and RLS.

## Success Criteria

- Customers can complete onboarding and start using the product on the first visit.
- Incoming leads are captured reliably and surfaced in a central dashboard.
- Reminder workflows reduce missed follow-ups and improve response consistency.
- Integrations such as email, Zapier/Make, and billing are enabled with minimal setup.

## Future Product Themes

- Expand automation templates for common service workflows.
- Add billing and subscription management for recurring revenue.
- Broaden integrations for email, messaging, appointment scheduling, and forms.
- Strengthen analytics and reporting for pipeline visibility.

## Principles

- Build simple, useful features first.
- Validate each addition with customer-facing workflows.
- Preserve a clean product experience while scaling the platform.
- Maintain trust by keeping security and data ownership transparent.
